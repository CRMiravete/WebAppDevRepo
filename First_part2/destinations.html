Location

Paris, France

Population:

68.043 M(as of 2023)

Latitude and Longitude:

Latitude: 48° 52' 25.5216'' N

Longitude: 2° 17' 41.8056'' E

Description:

One of the world's best-known commemorative monuments. The Arc de Triomphe is an iconic symbol of French national identity and took 30 years to build. The Tour de France bicycle race ends near it each year, and the annual military parade marking July 14—known both as French National Day and Bastille Day—begins its journey at the arch.

Description:

Flourishing to this day at the southeastern province of Yucatán, near the border to Quintana Roo lies the archeological site of Chichen Itzá (mayan ethimology that stands for "The city on the edge of the well of the Itzaes"). Officially nominated as one of the 7 Wonder of the World, this site bears a great cultural significance, as it was the capital of a large territory in the Yucatan peninsula, headed by the Mayapan league, an architecture with astrological knowledge of unpararlleled compare and magnificent traces of a once flourishing civilization.

Description:

Maglev Monorail Technology was one of those wonders which have gone dead silent in the contemporary era due to it's high cost production and, as such, scarce receptability of costumers; as a consecuence, no company or initative has dared to pick up the fragments of this intriguing tehnology, until today. The Yamanashi Maglev Test Center envisions the possibilities of transportation which said technology can benefit from if done correctly. What's breathtaking from this transport is that the monorail can travel at an mind - boggleling speed of 500 km/h (310.686 miles/h), and yet it feels like travelling at normal speed on any contemporary passenger train.
